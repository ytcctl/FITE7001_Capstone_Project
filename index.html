TokenHub: Tokenizing HKSTP Startups

A permissioned blockchain platform concept integrated with the Hong Kong Science & Technology Parks (HKSTP) Investment Platform to issue compliant security tokens representing fractional equity in HKSTP portfolio startups, and to enable secondary trading with atomic Delivery-versus-Payment (DvP) settlement.

1) Overview

Executive summary

  • Digitize fundraising workflows using smart contracts to reduce delay and operational friction.
  • Offer fractional ownership of HKSTP-backed startups using compliant security tokens.
  • Support a regulated secondary market to improve liquidity for investors.
  • Enable instant / near-instant settlement through atomic DvP (assets and payment settle together).

What makes it “HKSTP-ready”

  • Institutional maturity: use HKSTP’s rigorous vetting and ecosystem support.
  • Compliance-first: permissioned transfer controls + KYC/AML via licensed partners (VATP integration).
  • Investor experience: a portal for onboarding, wallet management, trading, settlement, and reporting.
  • Auditability: immutable on-chain events enable cap-table accuracy and reporting automation.

2) Problem & Motivation

Startup pain points

  • Extended fundraising timelines (often months).
  • Manual workflows (cap-table updates, subscriptions, reporting).
  • High transaction and operational costs.
  • Slow fund transfer and settlement processes.

Investor pain points

  • Limited liquidity in private markets (hard to exit early).
  • Lack of fractional ownership reduces access and flexibility.
  • Restricted access (often limited to accredited/institutional investors).
  • Information asymmetry and sporadic reporting.

3) Proposed Solution: TokenHub

Token layer

One token per startup, Ethereum-compatible, with compliance-aware transfer logic.

  • Security tokens representing fractional startup equity.
  • ERC-3643 (T-REX) considered for built-in compliance and identity integration.
  • Internal “safe-list” for operational addresses (e.g., treasury/custody/escrow).

Compliance & approvals

“Policy off-chain, enforcement on-chain” using per-transaction approvals.

  • KYC/AML handled via licensed partners (VATP integration via secure API).
  • Portal checks suitability, lock-ups, concentration caps, jurisdiction rules.
  • Compliance Service issues one-time signed approval (attestation) bound to each transfer.

Trading & settlement

Off-chain matching, on-chain atomic settlement (DvP).

  • Order-book model for secondary trading.
  • Atomic DvP: security token + tokenized cash move in the same transaction.
  • Event-driven reporting: transfers and settlements feed analytics & audit trails.

5) End-to-end Workflows (High-level)

Startup workflow (issuer side)

1

Onboard & set up tokenization

Startup joins the platform; cap-table and offering parameters are prepared for compliant issuance.

2

Issue security tokens (fractional equity)

Token contract is deployed/configured per startup; issuance controlled by offering rules.

3

Fundraising & investor communications

Investors subscribe through the portal; disclosures and updates are published via the dashboard.

4

Ongoing governance & reporting

On-chain events feed cap-table, audit logs, and regulator-ready reporting workflows.

Investor workflow (buy-side)

1

SSO + MFA registration

Account access through secure authentication, aligned with portal requirements.

2

KYC/AML via VATP integration

Identity verification, beneficial ownership checks, sanctions/PEP screening, and risk classification.

3

Funding & wallet management

Deposit/withdrawal flow (e.g., FPS); wallet creation and portfolio views.

4

Trading + atomic DvP settlement

Orders matched via order-book; settlement occurs on-chain with atomic asset/payment transfer.

KYC/AML capability checklist CDD + monitoring

Customer Due Diligence (CDD)

  • Identity verification with government-issued documents
  • Beneficial ownership determination (for legal entities)
  • Source-of-funds assessment
  • Sanctions and PEP screening (OFAC / UN / EU)
  • Risk-based investor classification

Ongoing monitoring

  • Real-time anomaly detection on token transfers
  • Pattern monitoring (e.g., rapid accumulation; profile-inconsistent trading)
  • Threshold-based reporting (example in plan: aggregated daily transfers > HK$500,000)
  • Periodic review of investor risk classification

6) Deliverables

A) Token trading marketplace & settlement

  • Investor portal: onboarding, funding/withdrawals (e.g., FPS), wallet management.
  • Real-time order book trading (off-chain matching).
  • Atomic DvP settlement with tokenized cash (stablecoin / tokenized deposit).
  • Portfolio views, statements, and real-time balance updates.

B) Secure token management module

  • Per-transaction approvals (e.g., signed attestation bound to from/to/amount/expiry/nonce).
  • Internal safe-list for operational movements (treasury/custody/escrow).
  • Multi-sig admin, emergency pause, monitoring, and audit readiness.
  • Runbook & security memo: key ceremonies, rotation, pause procedures.
  • Smart-contract compliance approach (ERC-3643 / permissioned transfers).
